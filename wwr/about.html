History

White water rafting companies began to emerge in the mid-20th century as outdoor adventure tourism grew in popularity. Originally, river rafting was used for exploration and transportation, but by the 1940s and 1950s, it became a recreational activity in places like the United States. As safety equipment improved and guided tours became more organized, commercial rafting companies were established to offer safe and thrilling experiences on rivers. Over time, the industry expanded worldwide, attracting adventure seekers and contributing to local tourism economies. Today, white water rafting companies provide professionally guided trips, prioritizing safety, environmental conservation, and memorable outdoor experiences.
